Possesives end on -to (attached to the possessor)
Accusative ends on -la
Dative ends on -ri
Locative ends on -miha
Adjectives end on -ka

Present Simple:
I eat - Ka yapa
no change

Past Simple:
I ate - Ka yapali
ends on -li

Future Simple:
I will eat; or
I am going to eat - Ka yapays
ends on -ys

A current continuous (in progress):
Work in progress - Warapay
ends on -pay

Present simple and present continuous are the same
Past simple and past continuous are the same
Future simple and future continuous are the same
