East Porpoistan Metro: Híjuro-ka Porpoistan Metro
-ka: direction adjective
Porpoistan is a noun modifier in compound so no -ka (otherwise it'd be Porpoistani/Porpoistanish)

I see my house
Ka erákoj ka-to aygawe-la
Ka -> I
Erájok -> see
Ka + -to -> my (I + possessive)
Aygawe + -la -> house (house + accusative)
